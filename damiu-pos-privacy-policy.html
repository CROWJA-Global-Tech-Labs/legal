Privacy Policy — DAMIU POS

Effective date: 17 April 2026
Developer: CROWJA Global Tech Labs
Contact: crowja.root@gmail.com

1. Introduction

DAMIU POS ("the App") is a point-of-sale application for refillable drinking water depot ("depot air minum isi ulang") businesses in Indonesia. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App handles, how it is stored, and who it is shared with.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data stored locally on your device

The App stores the following data only on your device, in a local SQLite database:

  • Customer records: name, phone number, address, photo of customer's building (optional), GPS coordinates (optional)
  • Product catalog: product name, prices (purchase/sale), color tag
  • Transaction records: date, customer, product, quantity, total amount, notes, gallon ownership type, returned-or-compensated status
  • Depot settings: shop name, address, phone, default pricing
  • Subscription status cache (Pro tier): purchase token, product ID, expiry timestamp

2.2 Device permissions requested

  • Camera — to take photos of customer buildings for delivery identification
  • Location (fine/coarse) — to tag customer addresses with GPS coordinates
  • Read Contacts — optional, to import existing contacts as customers
  • Write Contacts — optional, to save new customers back to the phone address book
  • Bluetooth — to print receipts to a thermal Bluetooth printer
  • Internet — required for AdMob advertising (free tier) and Google Play Billing (Pro tier)
  • AD_ID — used by Google AdMob for ad personalization on free tier

2.3 Data sent to third parties

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — Ad serving for free-tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers (Advertising ID), IP address, and app usage signals. See Google's Privacy Policy. Pro subscribers do not see ads and no ad-related data is transmitted.
  • Google Play Billing — Processes Pro subscription purchases. Payment details are handled entirely by Google Play; the App only receives the purchase token and product ID for Pro-status verification.

2.4 Data NOT collected

The App does not:

  • Upload customer, product, or transaction data to any server
  • Share your business data with third parties
  • Collect analytics or usage statistics beyond what AdMob requires for ad serving
  • Track your location in the background
  • Access your contacts without your explicit action (you must open the contact-picker screen)

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Because all business data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can:

  • Delete individual customers, products, or transactions from within the App
  • Clear all data by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage from Android Settings
  • Export your transaction history to CSV for backup at any time

4. Children's Privacy

DAMIU POS is intended for business use by adults (depot owners and staff). The App is not directed at children under 13. We configure AdMob with MAX_AD_CONTENT_RATING_G and block sensitive ad categories (gambling, alcohol, dating, etc.) for user safety.

5. Ad Category Controls

The following ad categories are blocked in AdMob console settings:

  • Online loans (pinjaman online / pinjol)
  • Gambling & casino
  • Cryptocurrency & speculative investments
  • Dating services
  • Sexual content
  • Tobacco & alcohol
  • Prescription medication
  • Weight-loss products
  • Political & religious content
  • Banking & conventional insurance

6. Security

Data is stored in Android's app-private storage (protected by the OS sandbox). We use HTTPS for all network communication with Google services. No user-provided data is transmitted in plain text.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the App. The effective date at the top will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at crowja.root@gmail.com.

1. Pendahuluan

DAMIU POS ("Aplikasi") adalah aplikasi point-of-sale untuk bisnis depot air minum isi ulang. Kebijakan Privasi ini menjelaskan data apa yang diproses, bagaimana data disimpan, dan kepada siapa data dibagikan.

2. Data yang Kami Kumpulkan

2.1 Data yang disimpan lokal di perangkat Anda

Aplikasi menyimpan data berikut hanya di perangkat Anda, dalam database SQLite lokal:

  • Data pelanggan: nama, nomor telepon, alamat, foto rumah (opsional), koordinat GPS (opsional)
  • Katalog produk: nama jenis air, harga (modal/jual), label warna
  • Riwayat transaksi: tanggal, pelanggan, jenis air, jumlah galon, total harga, catatan, status kepemilikan galon, status dikembalikan/ganti rugi
  • Pengaturan depot: nama depot, alamat, nomor telepon, harga default
  • Cache status langganan (tier Pro): purchase token, product ID, tanggal kedaluwarsa

2.2 Izin perangkat yang diminta

  • Kamera — mengambil foto rumah pelanggan untuk identifikasi pengiriman
  • Lokasi (fine/coarse) — menandai koordinat GPS alamat pelanggan
  • Baca Kontak — opsional, untuk import kontak HP menjadi pelanggan
  • Tulis Kontak — opsional, menyimpan pelanggan baru ke buku telepon
  • Bluetooth — mencetak struk ke printer thermal Bluetooth
  • Internet — untuk iklan AdMob (tier gratis) dan Google Play Billing (tier Pro)
  • AD_ID — digunakan Google AdMob untuk personalisasi iklan di tier gratis

2.3 Data yang dikirim ke pihak ketiga

Aplikasi terintegrasi dengan layanan pihak ketiga berikut:

  • Google AdMob — Menampilkan iklan untuk pengguna tier gratis. AdMob dapat mengumpulkan identifier perangkat (Advertising ID), alamat IP, dan sinyal penggunaan aplikasi. Lihat Kebijakan Privasi Google. Pelanggan Pro tidak melihat iklan dan tidak ada data iklan yang dikirim.
  • Google Play Billing — Memproses pembayaran langganan Pro. Detail pembayaran sepenuhnya ditangani Google Play; Aplikasi hanya menerima purchase token dan product ID untuk verifikasi status Pro.

2.4 Data yang TIDAK kami kumpulkan

Aplikasi tidak:

  • Mengupload data pelanggan, produk, atau transaksi ke server manapun
  • Membagikan data bisnis Anda ke pihak ketiga
  • Mengumpulkan statistik penggunaan di luar yang diperlukan AdMob
  • Melacak lokasi Anda di background
  • Mengakses kontak tanpa aksi eksplisit Anda (Anda harus membuka layar pemilih kontak)

3. Retensi & Penghapusan Data

Karena semua data bisnis disimpan lokal di perangkat Anda, Anda memiliki kontrol penuh. Anda dapat:

  • Menghapus pelanggan, produk, atau transaksi satu per satu dari dalam Aplikasi
  • Menghapus semua data dengan uninstall Aplikasi atau clear storage dari Pengaturan Android
  • Export riwayat transaksi ke CSV untuk backup kapanpun

4. Privasi Anak

DAMIU POS ditujukan untuk penggunaan bisnis oleh orang dewasa (pemilik dan staf depot). Aplikasi tidak ditujukan untuk anak di bawah 13 tahun. AdMob dikonfigurasi dengan MAX_AD_CONTENT_RATING_G dan kategori iklan sensitif (judi, alkohol, kencan, dll.) diblokir.

5. Kontrol Kategori Iklan

Kategori iklan berikut diblokir di pengaturan AdMob console:

  • Pinjaman online (pinjol)
  • Judi & casino
  • Cryptocurrency & investasi spekulatif
  • Layanan kencan
  • Konten seksual
  • Rokok & alkohol
  • Obat resep
  • Produk pelangsing
  • Konten politik & agama
  • Perbankan & asuransi konvensional

6. Keamanan

Data disimpan di storage privat Android (dilindungi sandbox OS). Semua komunikasi jaringan dengan Google menggunakan HTTPS. Tidak ada data pengguna yang dikirim dalam plain text.

7. Perubahan Kebijakan
